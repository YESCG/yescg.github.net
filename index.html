Why Are Computer Subjects Important in School?

In today's digital age, understanding computers and technology is essential for students. Computer subjects in school help build problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and prepare students for the rapidly evolving job market. They teach coding, digital literacy, and how to responsibly use technology.

Learning computer science from an early age empowers students with the skills needed for careers in software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and more.
